# AsLOV2 Workflow Fit

## Likely Fit

- `fast-no-cloning-screen`: useful when evaluating a known fusion design or a small candidate set
- `standard-construct-loop`: useful when tuning architecture, linker choice, localization, or host-specific performance

## Why This Is Separate

Workflow fit should be scored from step-level assumptions and downstream evidence, not from vibes about whether a tool feels "easy."

## Missing Evidence

- construct-specific failure rates
- typical sequencing strategy by construct complexity
- assay-dependent time-to-first-signal
