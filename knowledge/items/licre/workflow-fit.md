# LiCre Workflow Fit

## Likely Fit

- `fast-no-cloning-screen`: useful for reporter-based activity checks in yeast or mammalian cells
- `standard-construct-loop`: useful when recombination background, response time, or host-context effects require iteration

## Why

The main practical questions for `LiCre` are not just whether it works, but how much dark activity and host-specific behavior remain after construct integration.

## Missing Evidence

- quantitative dark-state leak
- preferred verification strategy for larger recombinase constructs
- typical time-to-readout in each host context
